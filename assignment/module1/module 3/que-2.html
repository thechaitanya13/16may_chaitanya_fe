What are tags and attributes in HTML?

answer  :Tags are the building blocks of HTML.They are usually written in pair tags.
example of tag    : <br>,<a>-> link tag
ATTRIBUTES   :Attributes provide additional information about an HTML element. They are specified within the opening tag of an element and consist of a name-value pair. The attribute name is followed by an equal sign (=)
Example of attributes   : href,style.
